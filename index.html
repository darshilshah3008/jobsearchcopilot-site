FAQ

Questions you're probably asking.

Why pay if it's open source?

The free repo is the engine. The paid template adds polish — pre-built role profiles, a buyer-facing setup guide, video walkthrough, Discord access, lifetime updates. Free is fine if you're a developer comfortable wiring it up. Paid is for getting it working in 10 minutes without reading docs.

What does it cost to actually run, beyond the one-time fee?

About $1-3/month in API costs, paid to OpenAI and Serper directly with your own keys. GitHub Actions and Gmail are free. There is no subscription to me — you bought it once.

Will this auto-apply to jobs for me?

No. We don't auto-apply, auto-message recruiters, or scrape platforms that prohibit it. The product surfaces jobs, scores them, drafts outreach you review and send, and tracks what you've applied to. You stay in control.

How accurate is the H1B sponsor flag?

Based on a curated list of ~150 companies that have publicly filed H1Bs in recent years. Strong signal but not a guarantee — companies can change policy. Always verify in the job description before investing time.

Does it work for non-engineering roles?

The infrastructure (search, scoring, tracker, H1B flagging) is role-agnostic. The pre-built profile templates are tuned for engineering. For other roles, edit the keywords in your profile.yaml (5 minutes) or wait for role-specific templates.

What about my privacy?

Everything runs in your own GitHub repo. Your resume, profile, and tracker live in your account. Your API keys go directly to OpenAI and Serper — we never see them. We have no server, no database, no account for you.
